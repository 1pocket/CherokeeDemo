Marina Services

A True Full-Service Marina

Cherokee Boat Dock is a full-service marina on the Tennessee River near Sugar Tree and Parsons. Pull up for gas, grab lunch and a cold drink, launch at the boat ramp, or make the dock your home base for the season.

  • Gas at the dock
  • Boat slips on the river
  • Boat ramp access
  • Grill with a full menu, beer & snacks
  • Marina store — souvenirs, ice cream & more
  • Full hook-up RV spots

Fuel types, slip availability and ramp details — call 731-847-3625 to confirm before you head out.

Tucked on the Tennessee River between Sugar Tree and Parsons in West Tennessee — easy to reach by truck and trailer, and a natural stop by boat. The surrounding stretch of river is known for bass, catfish and crappie fishing, with Natchez Trace State Park a short drive away.
