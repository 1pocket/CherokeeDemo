## 2. Facts that need owner confirmation

Reported by at least one directory but **not** confirmed by the business itself — verify before publishing as fact:

- **Hours of operation** — no listing shows current hours; likely seasonal. *(Ask owner.)*
- **Diesel fuel** — PredictWind lists both petrol and diesel; the Facebook description only says "gas." Confirm fuel types.
- **Slip count, sizes, transient dockage, and rates** — PredictWind lists berth sizes under 10m and 10–20m and <2m depth at low water, but this looks like templated data. Confirm.
- **RV rates, number of sites, and reservation process.**
- **Swimming area, water trampolines, inflatable slides, sandy beach** — mentioned in directory descriptions (Marinalife/search snippets); confirm they currently exist and are open to the public.
- **Live music and fishing tournaments** — mentioned by Seamagazine and Marinalife-style directories, which often use generated text. Do **not** publish an events schedule without owner confirmation; link to Facebook instead.
- **Restaurant name** — one directory calls it "The Cherokee Grill"; the business's own listing name is "Cherokee Marina and Grill." Confirm what they actually call the restaurant.
- **Menu items and prices** — no menu found on a public, reliable source. Zmenu has a listing ("Cherokee Heights boat dock") but it was inaccessible. Get a current menu (and food photos) from the owner.

## 3. Conflicting or possibly outdated information

- **Phone number:** Most sources (and the site owner brief) say **731-847-3625**, but one search-result snippet returned **731-847-2874**. The demo uses 731-847-3625; confirm with the owner whether the other number is old or a second line.
- **Boat rentals:** One directory description says "the dock offers boat rentals." This is exactly the kind of auto-generated claim the brief warns about — **not** included on the demo site. Confirm with owner.
- **Coordinates:** Marinas.com marina listing (35°43'52.5", −88°01'52.8") and its ramp listing (35°44'44.9", −88°02'08.9") differ slightly — likely marina vs. ramp locations.
- **Marinas.com listing is unclaimed** — the owner could claim it for free; good upsell talking point.

## 4. Questions to ask the owner

1. What are your current hours, and how do they change by season? Are you open year-round?
2. What fuel do you sell — gas only, or diesel too? Ethanol-free?
3. How many boat slips do you have? Covered/uncovered? Do you take transient (overnight) boaters? Rates?
4. How many RV sites? All full hook-up (water/electric/sewer)? Nightly/monthly rates? How do people reserve?
5. Can I get a copy of your current menu, and permission to publish it with prices?
6. What do you officially call the restaurant — Cherokee Marina and Grill, The Cherokee Grill, or something else?
7. Do you host live music or fishing tournaments? Should the site show a schedule or just point to Facebook?
8. Is there a public swimming area / beach, and any water toys (trampolines, slides) this season?
9. Do you rent boats or anything else (kayaks, pontoons)? (Directories claim this — true?)
10. Is the boat ramp free or paid? Public or customers-only?
11. Which phone number should be published — 731-847-3625, 731-847-2874, or both?
12. May I use photos from your Facebook page on the site? Which ones are your favorites?
13. Preferred email for the site — cherokeeboatdock@icloud.com or something else?
14. Would you like to claim your free Marinas.com and Google Business listings? (I can help.)
