# Cherokee Boat Dock — Demo Website

A speculative demonstration website for **Cherokee Boat Dock** (also listed as **Cherokee Marina and Grill**), 1748 Cherokee Heights Rd, Sugar Tree, TN 38380 — the marina on the Tennessee River near Sugar Tree and Parsons, TN.

> **Status:** Unofficial demo. Not commissioned or approved by the business. A disclaimer banner appears on the site itself.

## Files

- `index.html` — single-page site (hero, marina services, grill/menu, boat slips, RV sites, store, events, gallery, hours, location/map, contact)
- `css/styles.css` — all styling (mobile-first, responsive, sunset/river palette)
- `js/main.js` — mobile nav, scroll-reveal, footer year (site works without JS)
- `BUSINESS-INFO.md` — research notes: verified facts, unverified leads, conflicts, owner questions, sources, and photo-permission status

## Viewing

Open `index.html` directly in a browser, or serve locally:

```bash
python3 -m http.server 8000
# then visit http://localhost:8000
```

## Content honesty rules used

- Only publicly verified facts are stated as facts (gas, full menu, beer, slushies, ice cream, souvenirs, boat slips, full hook-up RV spots, boat ramp).
- No invented menus, prices, hours, rentals, or event schedules — those areas say "call to confirm" and link to the phone number or Facebook.
- No photos are used; every image is original placeholder artwork labeled for replacement pending owner-provided photos and permission.

See `BUSINESS-INFO.md` for the full list of items awaiting owner confirmation.
